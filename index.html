Welcome to UNIBTC, the universal source of local Bitcoin-for-cash traders e-mail lists. Everything you see on this website is open source and can be forked and pulled as you see fit. We would be happy if you wish to develop this website together with us.

The main idea is: UNIBTC wishes to have one confirmed representative and several unconfirmed traders in as many towns of the world as possible, so just one confirmed rep and s/he is quality checked ever so often and can be trusted and relied upon by people who wish to buy or sell bitcoins - they can also contact each other directly without fees if they wish to take the chance with an untested provider.

So, how to do? You just edit the wiki, send us an e-mail and decide whether you just want to buy or sell bitcoins or perhaps wants to become our rep in your town.

Advantages:

  • No online databases means increased security
  • Quality checks means increased safety and security for customers
  • One contact form, one network covering the bitcoin-for-cash market universally.

Recommendations for communicating safely:

  • IM: We recommend the cryptocat google chrome extension OR Pidgin with the Cypherpunks.ca OTR plugin
  • E-mail: Thunderbird with the Enigmail plugin, GnuPG, a Tormail account and a Boun.cr e-mail forwarding address to filter spam.

Never send bank details in an unencrypted message. This is common practice on some bitcoin exchange websites but can create a real security problem later on in the game.
